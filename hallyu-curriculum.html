How to play this record

This isn't a list of songs to like — it's a way to read a cultural movement. Each track pairs a guiding question with things to listen, watch, and read, then a short assignment that turns input into understanding. Work top to bottom (the order is historical) or jump to whatever's pulling at you.

🎤 Capstone — Curate your own B-side

You don't truly understand a movement until you can make an argument with it. Pick one of these and produce something a smart friend would actually want to read or watch:

  1. The 12-track mixtape essay. Build a playlist of 12 songs that tells the story of Hallyu from 1992 to now. Write one tight paragraph per track explaining why it earns its slot — the argument lives in the sequencing.
  2. The contradiction piece. Take one tension from the course (manufactured vs. authentic, soft power vs. exploitation, global vs. rooted) and write 800 words that refuses to resolve it cleanly. Hold both sides.
  3. The explainer reel. Script a 60–90 second video that explains why KPop Demon Hunters mattered to someone who thinks it's "just a cartoon." Use three facts from Track 12 and one idea from Track 11.
