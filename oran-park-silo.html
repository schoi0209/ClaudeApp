Silo Oran Park 실로 오란 파크

Silo Oran Park is a purpose-built industrial facility offering micro-warehouses and self-storage units at the heart of Oran Park's growing business precinct. Completed December 2025, with a 2-year post-completion rental guarantee. Past Silo projects in Wollongong, Huskisson and Nowra have all sold out — this is your chance.
